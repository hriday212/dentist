    About Dr.

    Dr. Saxena is a leading Orthodontist in Nerul, Mumbai, and the visionary founder of Dr. Saxena's Dental & Cosmetic Studio. With over 13 years of clinical excellence, Dr. Saxena is widely recognized for her advanced work in Invisalign treatment, cosmetic smile makeovers, and modern orthodontic solutions tailored to individual facial aesthetics.
